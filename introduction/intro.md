# How to start FuriLens with ease

## Input methods

You have three simple ways to send content into the FuriLens app and begin reading:

1. In another app, select a word or text, then tap “FuriLens” from the menu to open a pop-up.
2. In apps like a web browser, tap the share button. FuriLens will automatically parse and load the content.
3. Copy and paste text directly into the main app.

::: tip
⚡ Use (1) to keep your flow smooth and stay in your current app.

⚡ Use (2) when you're dealing with long web articles that are hard to select.

⚡ Use (3) when content comes from sources like Google Lens TTS or apps that don’t support FuriLens in the selection menu.
:::
